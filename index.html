Front List Intel

Publishing intelligence for emerging front list opportunities.

Front List Intel helps publishing professionals identify rising books before they become obvious market successes.

Each week, more than 800 newly released titles are reviewed across multiple genres and evaluated using a combination of ranking data, ratings activity, format availability, and publishing-specific opportunity signals.

The result is a curated report highlighting books demonstrating meaningful market momentum, along with the context needed to evaluate potential acquisition, scouting, rights, and publishing opportunities.

Who Uses Front List Intel?

  • Literary Agents seeking emerging authors and commercial opportunities
  • Editors and Publishers monitoring developing market trends
  • Rights Professionals evaluating translation, audio, and subsidiary rights opportunities
  • Scouts and Industry Professionals tracking notable titles before they reach broader visibility

What Makes It Different?

Most bestseller lists show what is already successful.

Front List Intel focuses on identifying books that are becoming successful.

The report combines objective market signals with publishing-specific analysis, helping professionals evaluate not only performance, but also potential opportunity.

Weekly Reports Include

  • Kindle rank and ratings activity analysis
