Methodology

How Front List Intel identifies emerging opportunities.

Weekly Review

Front List Intel reviews 800+ titles each week across tracked publishing categories, with a focus on books showing early commercial momentum.

Initial Screening

Titles are screened for format availability, Kindle performance, ratings count, reader rating, publication context, and other market signals.

Momentum Analysis

Titles are evaluated using current Kindle Store Rank, rank movement, and ratings count velocity.

Status Categories

Titles may be classified as New Entry, Active Momentum, Sustained Momentum, Cooling, or Archived.

Human Curation

Front List Intel is not a fully automated scrape. Final inclusion is shaped by human review, publishing context, and opportunity assessment.
